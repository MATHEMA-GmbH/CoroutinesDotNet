# Conclusion

::left::
**Advantages**
- Lower Overhead
- Ease of Use
- Thread-Safety (Coroutine 2.1.5)
- Ideal for I/O or Waiting Operations

::right::
**Disadvantages**
- No Real Parallelism
- Manual Tick Usage (Coroutine 2.1.5)
- Limited to Asynchronous Flows
